Efficient Contact Tracing in Baguio

Each individual in Baguio will have a QR Code. This QR code system contains information that is essential to contact tracing. This can be saved in smartphones or can be printed and be used as a Pass for the residents of Baguio City.

The City of Baguio will create an ordinance, requiring High Risk Areas such as Markets, Malls, Banks, Hospitals, Checkpoints and even transportation system to assign scanners at the entrance before an individual will enter their vicinity . The scanners can use their smartphones to log in people in real time coming to their establishments where this will be listed and be centralized in the data system of the City Health Office. This will eliminate the usage of logbooks and the gaps in tracing for the contact tracers.

Read More

The application system, will also be designed to inform the close contacts of a positive COVID -19 patient via text message. This can be done by getting the travel history of the COVID patient for the past two weeks using the system.  The data system, can also organize and line list the close contacts of the COVID patient according to barangays. This list will be used as a guide  by the health teams and other partner sectors of each barangays for close contact monitoring.

Eventually, this can help in gatekeeping patients to avoid overwhelming hospital facilities.

Another feature of the application system is informing immediately the establishment If a positive COVID -19 patient has been to their premises. This is to help in the immediate disinfection of the establishment so as to avoid infection of the disease.

If this system works, Baguio City can be a model community for an efficient contact tracing system using an Application system which can be adapted by the other provinces in the Philippines to control the rise of COVID -19 .

How will we integrate the Contact Tracing Application in the Contact Tracing System of Baguio City ?

This Contact Tracing Application will be used particularly to identify close contacts of a Positive COVID 19 patient immediately lessening the delays, in the manual searching of logbooks of the contact tracing team. Since this application will note the 14 day travel history  of the patient, there will be less gaps in the investigation of the  contact tracing team to locate other close contacts. The application aims to involve the residents of Baguio more to the COVID-19 situation in order to ease the burden of not knowing whether they are close contacts or not. Eventually , this can help people realize the notion of self-quarantine and seeking medical attention if ever they carry the symptoms of COVID-19
